Academics

Rigour, taught with joy.

From phonics in Nursery to AI electives in Class XII — how learning is built, stage by stage.

Learning stages

One school, three journeys

Primary · Nursery–V

Play-led, phonics-based early learning that moves into inquiry projects. No formal examinations until Class III; progress is shared through portfolios and conversations.

Middle · VI–VIII

Subject depth begins: integrated science, a second language (Sanskrit, French, or German), coding for all, and the first inter-house academic championships.

Senior · IX–XII

Board preparation with breadth intact: Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams, 14 electives, career counselling from Class IX, and university mentoring.

Senior school

Streams & signature electives

Science

Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, with electives in Applied AI & Data Science, Astronomy, and Biotechnology. Weekly lab immersion in the Sen STEM Centre.

Commerce

Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, with electives in Entrepreneurship and Financial Markets. Students run Parikrama, the school's registered student company.

Humanities

History, Political Science, Psychology, Sociology, with electives in Classical Literature and Film Studies. Home of the award-winning Model UN programme.

Beyond marks: every senior student completes a two-year independent research project, presented to an external jury at the annual Nalanda Colloquium.

Facilities

Spaces built for learning

Sen STEM Centre

Robotics, maker lab, rooftop observatory, and a 3D-printing studio.

Two libraries

68,000 volumes, digital archives, and silent study decks for seniors.

Sports complex

Olympic pool, 400 m track, indoor courts, and a cricket oval.

Arts conservatory

Music rooms, dance studios, a kiln, and the 400-seat amphitheatre.
