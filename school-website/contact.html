Getting here

Visiting the Revelganj campus

By rail

Chhapra Junction is 8 km away — autos and e-rickshaws run to Godna Road all day. Revelganj station (local halts) is a 10-minute walk.

By bus

All NH-19 services stop at Revelganj bus stand, five minutes' walk from the visitor gate.

School transport

18 GPS-tracked buses cover Revelganj, Chhapra, Siwan, and Sonepur. Route queries: transport@jdsps.example.edu.in.
