Virtual classroom

Your classroom,
anywhere.

Scheduled video lessons and voice doubt-clearing hours over secure, school-managed Google Meet rooms.

How it works

Learning doesn't stop at the school gate

Every class from VI to XII has a scheduled virtual room for remote lessons, doubt-clearing sessions, and guest lectures. Sessions run on Google Meet through school-managed accounts, so only verified @nalandaps students and staff can join.

Video sessions are for lessons and labs; voice-only sessions are lighter on bandwidth and used for doubt-clearing hours and mentoring calls. Both types appear in the schedule below.

Safety & privacy: all sessions are hosted by a teacher, joining requires your school Google account, recordings are stored on the school drive for 30 days, and personal phone numbers are never shared. Report any concern to esafety@nalandaps.example.edu.in.

Before you join

  • Sign in with your school Google account (rollno@nalandaps).
  • Keep your microphone muted unless invited to speak.
  • Cameras on for lessons, optional for doubt-clearing hours.
  • Use the raise-hand feature — the chat is for questions only.
  • Recordings and screenshots by students are not permitted.
  • Having trouble? Call the IT helpdesk: +91 11 4000 0099 (7 AM–5 PM).
