About us

Sixty-two years,
one brass bell.

The story of a school that grew from a two-room bungalow into three campuses — without losing sight of the child in the front row.

Our mission

Rigour with warmth, tradition with imagination

Founded in 1962 by educationist Dr. Kamala Sen, Nalanda Public School began with 48 students in a two-room bungalow and a conviction: that a great school teaches children how to think, not what to think.

Today we educate 4,800 students across three campuses — yet class sizes stay small, teachers know every child by name, and the bungalow's original brass bell still opens every school year.

From the Principal's desk

"Parents often ask what makes Nalanda different. My answer is always the same: we measure ourselves not by the ranks our students earn, but by the questions they dare to ask. Ranks follow."

Dr. Meera Krishnan
Principal, Senior School · M.Ed., Ph.D. (Education, DU)

What we value

The four pillars

Scholarship

Deep learning over rote memorisation — in every subject, at every age.

Integrity

Doing the right thing when no one is watching. Our honour code is student-written.

Compassion

Service is part of the timetable, not an afterthought — from Class III upwards.

Courage

On stage, on the field, and in the classroom: we celebrate the brave attempt.

Our journey

Six decades of firsts

1962

Dr. Kamala Sen opens the school with 48 students and 4 teachers in Chanakyapuri.

1975

Senior secondary wing inaugurated; first batch sits the board examinations.

1988

The Dwarka campus opens, bringing the Nalanda approach to west Delhi.

2003

Noida campus founded; the three-campus house system is born.

2015

The Sen Centre for STEM opens with robotics, astronomy, and maker labs.

2024

Applied AI & Data Science elective launched with IIT-Delhi outreach mentors.

2026

4,800 students, 210+ faculty, and 27,000 alumni across the world.

Belonging

The house system

The house of energy and initiative. Reigning sports champions.

Varuna

The house of depth and reflection. Debate and quiz powerhouse.

Prithvi

The house of stewardship. Runs the eco club and campus farm.

Vayu

The house of expression. Home of the annual production.
