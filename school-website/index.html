Why Nalanda

An education that goes beyond the syllabus

Three campuses, one philosophy: every child deserves rigour in the classroom, room to play, and a stage to find their voice.

Academic depth

A CBSE curriculum enriched with Olympiad training, research projects, and electives from AI to classical literature — taught by 210+ faculty, 40% with postgraduate research degrees.

Sports & the arts

Sixteen acres of fields, an Olympic-size pool, a 400-seat amphitheatre, and dedicated conservatories for music, dance, and fine art. Every student plays, performs, or paints.

Character & community

A house system that builds belonging, a student council with real responsibility, and 20,000+ hours of community service logged by our students every year.
