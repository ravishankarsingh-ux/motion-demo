Admissions 2026–27

Begin the journey.

Applications are open for Nursery to Class XI across all three campuses. Applications close 15 August 2026.

How it works

Four steps to joining Nalanda

1

Apply online

Submit the inquiry form below or the full application through the admissions office. No application fee for Nursery–KG.

2

Campus visit

Join a guided tour and an informal conversation with the admissions team. Children are welcome.

3

Interaction

Age-appropriate interaction for the child (Classes I+) and a meeting with parents. No written tests below Class VI.

4

Offer & enrolment

Offers are emailed within ten working days. Fee submission and document verification complete enrolment.
